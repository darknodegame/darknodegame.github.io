What makes it tick

No guessing, ever

Every Vector is verified solvable by deduction alone. Stuck means you missed a clue — never that the board cheated.

Line-of-sight hints

Beyond plain adjacency: hints that see down rows and diagonals, count clusters, and track runs. A deeper deduction space than classic sweeping.

Scan Logs & Vectors

Hand-tuned packs of levels that ramp from cold-boot basics to knotty multi-hint boards. Clear one to unlock the next.

A toolkit, not luck

Surge Guard, Cue, and Threat Map — limited-charge helpers for the tight spots when a board turns hostile.

Built for the phone

Portrait, one-handed, tap to reveal and long-press to mark. Boards cap at a comfortable size so nothing gets unplayable.

A story in the signal

Story panels break between Vectors — a handler on the line, a breach that wasn't in the test plan. The grid is the mission; the plot is why.
